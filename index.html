NEW SINGLE from RUN DOWNHILL

ROCK AND ROLL AIN'T A RHYTHM, the first single from RDh's upcoming summer album, is now available on Spotify, YouTube, and everywhere music is streamed on the web!

Written, produced, recorded and performed by T.J. Troy, this song features some amazing guest musicians, including Adam Levy, Nate LaPointe, Will Rainier, and the Rock and Roll Chorus Singers - Jen Garrett, Steve Gregoropoulos, Susanna Jivotovski, Nate LaPointe, Adam Levy, Heather Lockie, Will Rainier, Alex Wand.

Staying in the milieu of past RDh releases, this song features incredible artwork from the very talented Toronto-based comics artist CGhirardo, skillfully weaving art and music into a motion comic video.

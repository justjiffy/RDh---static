JUST RELEASED...

SOMETHING WILL COME OF THIS is the latest single from Run Downhill’s upcoming album nineteen/twenty, and comes with 3 brand new B-Sides, now available on Spotify, YouTube, and everywhere music is streamed on the web!

Written, produced, recorded and performed by T.J. Troy, the song’s eerie, cinematic arrangement supports T.J.’s haunting voice and transports the listener to lonely road in an abandoned city. Its solitary plea for meaning amidst loss and hunger is a stark reminder of our contemporary world.

Staying in the milieu of past RDh releases, each song on this new release features its own motion comic video, further expanding the fictional world of Run Downhill and its ever-expanding cast of outcasts and lost souls. With incredible artwork from the very talented Toronto-based comics artist CGhirardo, along with artwork from RDh cover artist Scott Angle, these motion comic videos are a perfect companion to these songs, available for all on RDh’s YouTube channel.
